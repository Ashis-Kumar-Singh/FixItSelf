Acer HDD Repair Guide

Step 1

  • Turn off the laptop and unplug it.
  • Flip the laptop over.
  • Remove four 3mm screws.

Step 2

  • Remove the back case carefully.
  • Undo the clips along the perimeter.

Step 3

  • Unplug the battery before handling the HDD.

Step 4

  • Remove four screws from the HDD bracket.

Step 5

  • Place the HDD in the bracket and secure with screws.

Step 6

  • Attach the SATA connector to the HDD.

Step 7

  • Slide the HDD into place and secure it.

Step 8

  • Connect the hard drive cable to the motherboard.
