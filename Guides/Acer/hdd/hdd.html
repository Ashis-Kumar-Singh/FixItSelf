Acer HDD Repair Guide

Step 1: Preparation

  • ✅ Turn off the laptop and unplug it.
  • ✅ Flip the laptop over.
  • ✅ Remove four 3mm screws from the back panel.

Step 2: Remove Back Case

  • ✅ Carefully remove the back case.
  • ✅ Undo the clips along the perimeter gently.

Step 3: Disconnect Battery

  • ✅ Unplug the battery before handling the HDD.

Step 4: Remove HDD Bracket

  • ✅ Remove four screws securing the HDD bracket.

Step 5: Insert New HDD

  • ✅ Place the new HDD in the bracket.
  • ✅ Secure it with screws.

Step 6: Connect SATA Cable

  • ✅ Connect the SATA cable to the HDD.

Step 7: Secure the HDD

  • ✅ Slide the HDD back into place.
  • ✅ Secure it with screws.

Step 8: Reassemble and Finish

  • ✅ Reconnect the hard drive cable to the motherboard.
  • ✅ Reassemble the back cover and secure screws.
