Acer RAM Replacement Guide

Step 1

  • Turn off the laptop and unplug it from the charging cord.
  • Flip the laptop over so the backside is facing up.
  • Use a Phillips #0 screwdriver to remove the back panel screws.

Step 2

  • Use your fingers or an opening tool to remove the back case by separating it at the seam.
  • Work your way gently around the perimeter to undo the clips.
  • Remove the back case completely.

Step 3

  • Locate the RAM modules in your laptop. They are typically covered by a thin metal shield.
  • If your laptop has a battery that's accessible, disconnect it before proceeding.

Step 4

  • Gently pull the retention clips on both sides of the RAM module outward.
  • The RAM module will pop up at a 30-degree angle.

Step 5

  • Carefully grasp the RAM module by its edges.
  • Pull the module straight out of the slot.
  • Set the old RAM module aside in an anti-static bag if you plan to keep it.

Step 6

  • Take your new RAM module, holding it by the edges.
  • Align the notch on the new RAM module with the ridge in the RAM slot.
  • Insert the RAM at a 30-degree angle, making sure it's fully seated in the slot.

Step 7

  • Press down on the RAM module until the retention clips snap into place.
  • You should hear a click when the module is properly secured.
  • Verify that the retention clips are properly engaged.
