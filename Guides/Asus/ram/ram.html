Asus RAM Replacement Guide

Step 1: Power Down & Prepare

  • ✅ Shut down your laptop and unplug the power adapter.
  • ✅ Turn the laptop over onto a flat surface.

Step 2: Access the RAM Panel

  • ✅ At the top center on the back of the device, locate the small square panel.
  • ✅ Use a Phillips screwdriver to remove the two screws securing the panel.

Step 3: Remove the Old RAM

  • ✅ Ground yourself from electrostatic discharge by touching a metal object or using anti-static gloves.
  • ✅ Push apart the latches on either side of the slot holding the RAM in place.
  • ✅ Use firm pressure if needed. You will hear the RAM pop out of the slot.
  • ✅ Carefully remove the RAM stick and place it on an anti-static surface.

Step 4: Insert the New RAM

  • ✅ Hold the new RAM by its edges, with the gold contacts facing down.
  • ✅ Align the RAM properly with the slot and insert it at an angle.
  • ✅ Apply gentle pressure until the RAM clicks securely into place.
  • ✅ Once secured, screw the panel door back on.
  • ✅ Power on the device and ensure the RAM is recognized.
