Asus SSD Replacement Guide

Step 1 SSD

  • Disconnect the ASUS laptop from the external power supply before starting.
  • Place the laptop on a flat table with its back facing up.

Step 2

  • Slide the latch on the right side of the battery on the back of the laptop to the right to the unlocked position.

Step 3

  • With your left hand, slide the latch on the left side of the battery to the left and hold it.
  • With your right hand, slide the battery out of the battery compartment and place the battery away from the laptop.

Step 4

  • Remove the two screws on the bottom of the laptop using a Phillips #0 screwdriver.

Step 5

  • Slide the hard disk compartment cover towards you with both hands and remove it from the laptop.

Step 6

  • Remove the four screws from the hard drive support arm using a Phillips #0 screwdriver.

Step 7

  • Lift the plastic sheet on the left side of the hard drive slightly by hand.
  • Remove the hard drive and its support from the hard drive compartment.

Step 8

  • Remove the four screws on the side of the hard disk support using a Phillips #0 screwdriver.
