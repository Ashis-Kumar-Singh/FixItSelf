HP HDD Replacement Guide

Step 1: Disconnect Power

  • ✅ Make sure the laptop is turned off and the power cable is disconnected.
  • ✅ Avoid hardware damage by turning off the system before proceeding.

Step 2: Access Back Panel

  • ✅ Turn the laptop over to access the backside.
  • ✅ Confirm that the laptop is an HP Pavilion DV5T-1000.

Step 3: Remove Battery

  • ✅ Slide the battery lock to the left below the battery compartment.
  • ✅ Remove the battery carefully to avoid hardware damage.

Step 4: Locate HDD Compartment

  • ✅ Identify the hard drive compartment located at the top left of the laptop.

Step 5: Remove Cover Screws

  • ✅ Use a Phillips #0 screwdriver to remove the two screws securing the cover.

Step 6: Expose the HDD

  • ✅ Lift the cover to expose the hard drive.

Step 7: Loosen HDD Connection

  • ✅ Gently tug the black tabs on both ends to loosen the hard drive.
  • ✅ Be cautious while handling the HDD cable connected to the motherboard.

Step 8: Disconnect HDD

  • ✅ Firmly hold the black plug and the hard drive to detach them safely.

Step 9: Remove Bracket

  • ✅ Use a Phillips #0 screwdriver to remove the four screws securing the bracket.
  • ✅ Carefully remove the metal bracket from the hard drive.
