HP RAM Replacement Guide

Step 1: Flip the Laptop

  • ✅ Flip the laptop over so the bottom is facing up and the external battery is at the top.

Step 2: Remove Battery

  • ✅ Use your thumbs to slide the battery locks towards the center.
  • ✅ Pop the battery out gently to avoid any damage.

Step 3: Remove Rubber Stoppers

  • ✅ Slowly peel off the two rubber stoppers at the top corners.
  • ✅ Be cautious to avoid stripping the adhesive.

Step 4: Remove Back Plate

  • ✅ Use a Phillips #00 screwdriver to remove the 12 screws.
  • ✅ Carefully lift the back plate using an opening tool.
  • ✅ You will hear a "click" when the clips are released.

Step 5: Remove Existing RAM

  • ✅ Locate the RAM sticks in the bottom right section.
  • ✅ Push both side arms outwards to release the RAM.
  • ✅ Slide the RAM out at a 45-degree angle gently.

Step 6: Install New RAM

  • ✅ Carefully insert the new RAM sticks into the slots.
  • ✅ Align them properly and slide them in gently.
  • ✅ Press down until the latches click into place to secure them.
