Hp RAM Replacement Guide

Step 1

  • Flip the laptop over so the bottom is facing up and the external battery is at the top.

Step 2

  • Use your thumbs to slide the battery locks towards the center and pop the battery out.

Step 3

  • Slowly peel off the two rubber stoppers found at the top corners of the laptop.
  • This will expose two Phillips #00 10 mm screws required to remove the back plate.
  • Be careful not to peel off the rubber stoppers too quickly as it could cause the adhesive on the rubber stopper to strip off.

Step 4

  • Use a Phillips #00 screwdriver to remove the twelve 10 mm screws (from the twelve holes shown) that secure the bottom case.
  • Use an opening tool to carefully separate the back case from the chassis, working around all four sides.
  • You will hear a "click" as you release the clips and remove it.

Step 5

  • The RAM sticks can be found in the bottom right section of the laptop.
  • Push both side arms holding the RAM stick in place outwards.
  • The RAM sticks will "pop" up at a 45-degree angle when released from the arms.
  • Slide the RAM sticks out of the socket gently.

Step 6

  • Carefully slide the new replacement RAM sticks into their slots.
  • Make sure to align the edge of the RAM sticks with the slots to avoid damaging both.
  • Gently push the RAM sticks downward until the latches click into place to secure them.
